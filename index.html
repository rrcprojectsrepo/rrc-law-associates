About Us

RRC Law Associates is a boutique litigation firm defined by precision, principle, and courtroom strength. Established in 2025, the firm carries forward the legacy of its founder, Durai Balasundaram, M.A., B.L., Senior Advocate - A first-generation lawyer and 1964 enrollee, he built a formidable reputation in criminal and civil litigation through analytical rigor and unwavering ethics. Today, we represent clients across District Courts, High Courts, and the Supreme Court of India, combining deep legal scholarship with disciplined, contemporary strategy to protect our clients' rights with authority.

We specialize in representing NRIs, overseas citizens, and foreign nationals, serving as a transparent bridge to the Indian judicial system. Our team provides comprehensive management of cross-border interests —including property disputes, inheritance, and matrimonial matters—leveraging technology to ensure international clients remain informed and protected regardless of distance.

By combining meticulous preparation with a "boots on the ground" presence, we navigate the nuances of Indian law to offer our global clientele peace of mind. Whether resolving ancestral land issues or complex regulatory hurdles, we deliver high-stakes representation that upholds your interests with the integrity and authority of our founding legacy.
