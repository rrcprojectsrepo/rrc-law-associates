The Remote Advocacy Process

Zero-Travel Legal Solutions

We serve as your definitive legal anchor in Tamil Nadu, managing every aspect of your case while you remain with your family and career abroad.

01
Video Consultation

Initial strategy session via Zoom or WhatsApp, scheduled according to your local time zone (Singapore, UAE, USA, UK).

02
Document Review

Digital audit of your current deeds, Patta, Chitta, and EC to identify discrepancies or administrative gaps.

03
Power of Attorney Setup

Guidance on drafting, embassy attestation, and local adjudication of POA to allow us to represent you legally.

04
Ground Verification

Physical site inspection by our team in Tamil Nadu, including boundary checks and revenue office record cross-matching.

05
Case Filing / Negotiation

Initiating litigation in District or High Courts, or engaging in high-stakes negotiations to resolve disputes out of court.

06
Monthly Updates

Transparent reporting through monthly case logs and digital updates, ensuring you are never out of the loop.
